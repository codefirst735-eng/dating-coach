Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, use our AI coaching services, or upload screenshots for analysis. This may include your name, email address, and the content of your communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, to process your requests, and to communicate with you. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Screenshot Analysis

When you upload screenshots for analysis, they are processed in real-time by our AI. We prioritize your privacy and do not permanently store these images on our servers after the analysis is complete. We strongly recommend blurring any sensitive personal information before uploading.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@relationshipforhumans.com.
