The Playbook

How to extract maximum value from the Relationship for Humans ecosystem.

1

Mastering the AI Coach

The AI Coach is not a therapist. It is a strategist. To get the best results, you need to provide clear, raw data.

Best Practices:
  • ➤ Be Specific: Don't just say "She's acting weird." Say "She takes 4 hours to reply and gives one-word answers."
  • ➤ Provide Context: Mention how long you've been dating, who texted last, and the general vibe of the relationship.
  • ➤ Ask for Action: Ask "What should I text back?" or "How do I handle this specific situation?"
2

Decoding Texts

Texting is a minefield of subtext. Our Analyzer reads between the lines to reveal the hidden power dynamics.

How to Use:
  • ➤ Upload Clear Images: Ensure the text is readable.
  • ➤ Protect Privacy: Always blur out names, phone numbers, and photos before uploading.
  • ➤ Analyze the Result: The AI will give you an assessment of the "Frame" (who is in control) and a recommended reply.
